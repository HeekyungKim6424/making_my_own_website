Quidditch postponed due to corona

Due to Corona, Quidditch,
which was scheduled to be held in April, has been postponed until July.
There is no guarantee that it will be held in July,
and it is expected that there will be some limitations.
Until then, all use of the stadium is prohibited.
I fully understand your disappointment, and I also regret it.
Each dormitory leader would like to resume the practice using some open spaces.
Thank you.

April 2021
Principal Hogwarts Minerva McGonagall.
